spomenik grant

$50k–$100k for people building things that matter. no strings attached. awarded in 3-month cycles.

~ what is this.

spomenik is a grant for builders, researchers, and creators working on ambitious projects. the name comes from the yugoslav monuments — massive, brutalist structures built to honor something worth remembering. this grant is the same idea: back people doing work worth remembering.

~ how it works.

every 3 months, we select a small number of recipients. each one gets between $50k and $100k, depending on the project and what's needed. there's no equity taken, no board seats, no strings. you get the money, you do the work.

~ who is this for.

people building at the edges. technical founders, independent researchers, artists pushing boundaries, community builders doing something real. you don't need a company, a degree, or a track record. you need a project and the drive to see it through.

~ what we look for.

ambition. clarity of thought. a project that wouldn't exist without you. we don't care about pedigree — we care about what you're building and why.

~ how to apply.

send an email to natochi@platan.us with: your name, what you're building, why it matters, and how much you need. keep it short. we'll get back to you within 2 weeks.
