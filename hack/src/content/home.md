## About Platanus Hack

Platanus Hack is the yearly hackathon organized by [Platanus](https://platan.us), a Latin American startup accelerator. Previous editions:

- **Hack 25** — 200 hackers, hosted at Buk offices, Santiago, Chile.
- **Hack 24** — 120 hackers, hosted at Fintual offices, Santiago, Chile.

For 2026, the hackathon expands into a LATAM-wide tour with stops in five countries.
